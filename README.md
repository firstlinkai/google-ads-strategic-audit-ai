# Google Ads Performance Audit & Strategic Insights 📊

A professional-grade performance audit and strategic analysis for **Sahara Trailers**. This repository demonstrates a hybrid workflow: combining manual data extraction from the Google Ads UI with AI-powered data interpretation to deliver executive-level marketing intelligence.

---

## 🚀 Project Context
This project was developed as a "Value-First" audit for a digital marketing agency. When technical limitations (Read-Only access and API restrictions) prevented a fully automated n8n workflow, I pivoted to a manual-to-AI hybrid model to ensure the client received timely, high-impact insights.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Source:** Google Ads (AUD Account)
* **Data Processing:** Google Sheets (Manual ETL & Aggregation)
* **Analysis Engine:** Google Gemini (Strategic Narrative Generation)
* **Visualization:** Google Slides (Data Storytelling)

## 💡 Strategic Insights (The "Story" in the Data)

### 1. The Efficiency Leader
The **Performance Max - NQ** campaign was identified as the account's "Conversion Engine," responsible for nearly **90% of total volume** (179.01 conversions) at a highly efficient **A$11.69 CPA**. 

### 2. Creative Resonance
Despite rising costs in February, the **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** grew by **29%**. This indicates that the current ad creative is successfully cutting through market noise and capturing higher-intent traffic.

### 3. Optimization & Reallocation
The audit revealed that the **Search - Non Brand (Caravans)** campaign represented the highest spend (**A$2,169.34**) with minimal conversion output (4 conversions). This led to a direct strategic recommendation to reallocate this budget to the Performance Max "Efficiency Leader."
